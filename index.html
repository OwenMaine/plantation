About Us

Creating Beautiful Outdoor Living Spaces Since 1990

At Plantation Patterns, we're passionate about transforming outdoor spaces into beautiful, comfortable living areas. Located in Calera, AL, we've established ourselves as a leading manufacturer of premium outdoor furniture and accessories. Our commitment to quality craftsmanship and innovative design has made us a trusted name in the outdoor furniture industry.

We specialize in creating durable, stylish outdoor furniture that withstands the elements while maintaining its beauty. From elegant patio sets to comfortable cushions and accessories, each piece is crafted with attention to detail and a focus on both aesthetics and functionality. Our products are designed to enhance your outdoor living experience, whether you're hosting a gathering or simply enjoying a peaceful moment in your garden.
